The Hulrar tribe is the 2nd-strongest tribe and the strongest voice for Talenta unification beyond the limited confederation preferred by the dominant Abramams, and the dissolution favored by social Darwinist and traditionalist "Holy" Uldra Sindrekel. 996YK: The Last War ends with the Treaty of Thronehold (996-11-11).

Kaerlen's name means "Good Ruby" (Races of Stone 49). He has a fondness for non-Gnomish women, especially
Talenta Halflings. Not that he's averse to Gnomish women or "tall ladies" at all. He has met few Dwarven
women.

Member of House Sivis (ECS236) and its Speaker's Guild (DrM73), employed as needed for the campaign
in public. Possibly working for House Sivis as an Agent (DrM14).

Sivis members in good standing can use a Message Station for free, and other services at 50% cost.
Sivis families are also prone to giving each other aid - not quite Favored in House, but helpful (DrM72).
