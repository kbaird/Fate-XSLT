Description Slim spent most of World War II conducting test flights of America's most advanced airframes. Near the close of the war Slim was selected to fly Atomic Robo's unit deep behind German lines as the vanguard of Operation Paperclip. Slim would later become one of the original Science Agents of Tesladyne. His personal expertise in the cutting edge of aeronautics technology and the limits of the human body would serve the firm well in its early days.
