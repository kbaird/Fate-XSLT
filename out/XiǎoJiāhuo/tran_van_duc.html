NameTrần Văn Đức / 陈文德 / "Ancient Culture Virtue" (Đức)
Story Đức was part of the unsuccessful resistance against the Túyàngan invaders in the neighboring nation of Đại Nam (大南, "Big South"). He has since relocated, along with a sizeable Đạinamese community, attempting to rebuild in Kausao City, with the eventual dream of liberating Đạinam from the 图样人 devils. (文 is a very typical male Đạinamese middle name, and the custom is to refer to one by personal name, like Đức). I see the Đạinamese community in Kausao as having several parallels to the Tauron community in Caprica City. The Browncoat analogy applies more to Đức and some others as individuals.
