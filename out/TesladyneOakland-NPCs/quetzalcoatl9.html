In addition, a combination of the proper equipment, the right conditions, and a precise command sequence on the hosting computer system (in other words, a compel on Divine Simulation) can force you into a corporeal state. This causes you to lose the benefits of this stunt and the use of the Hover skill until the end of the scene, at which time you revert to incorporeality again.
