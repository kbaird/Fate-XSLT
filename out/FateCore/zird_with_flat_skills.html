The Power of Deduction
Once per scene you can spend a fate point (and a few minutes of observation) to make a special Study roll representing your potent deductive faculties. For each shift you make on this roll you discover or create an aspect, on either the scene or the target of your observations, though you may only invoke one of them for free.
I've Read About That!
You’ve read hundreds—if not thousands—of books on a wide variety of topics. You can spend a fate point to use Study in place of any other skill for one roll or exchange, provided you can justify having read about the action you’re attempting.
