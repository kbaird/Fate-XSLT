Phase 1: ChildhoodAspects: Chinese Diaspora 2035: Tír Tairngire secedes from SSC; CFS kicked out of UCAS 2036: TT invades northern CFS to Redding 2041-2044: Nationalist War in China 2042: Liu's parents emigrate from Beijing to TT 2046-05-08 17:15:12 -0700: Liu born in Cara'Sir (Portland), TT Both parents are 1st Generation Han Chinese Elves who emigrated from greater Beijing to Tír Tairngire, motivated by the welcoming-to-metahumans rhetoric of TT's founding and the unrest in China at that time. Under Tír Tairngire's autocratic government, Martin was a member of the Rinelle ke'Tesrae's moderate/liberal wing, favoring democratic elections and a reduction/elimination of the formal caste system. Since the elections of 2065, he eschews revolutionary action in favor of "within the system" reforms. He has no particular admiration for the previous unelected Princes, and adheres to no significant racism directed at either metatypes or ethnic backgrounds, thereby opposing the Elf supremacist factions in the Rinelle [LOP7-8/SofNA142-3]. His family spoke mainly 普通话 at home. His true native language is now Sperethiel, and he has a strong non-native command of English.

    Spellcaster notes:
    Keep it loose. Just do the Four Actions via the new Magic Skill, and give narrative
    description according to the particular Shadowrun spell desired. I figure a pretty loose
    definition of the stunt is OK, since it requires a completely new skill (Magic), burns a
    Stunt in addition to the required Magician extra, AND requires in-game narrative
    justification from the player.

    The particulars of each spell will be largely up to the caster. Some specific patterns
    (such as Attacks via mana spells being Opposed with Will; physical spells with Physique)
    will be fairly typical.

    Spirit Summoner notes:
    Can petition the Celestial Bureaucracy for magical favors. Again, keep it loose, and use
    the new Magic Skill. Maybe start with Creating the "Owes Service to [me]" Advantage on a
    specific Spirit that provides Active Opposition with its highest-rated Skill. Spending an
    action to Create more Advantages simply adds more invocations (i.e., services). The
    narrative particulars depend on the specific type of Spirit, etc.

    Drain:
    Use the options for Extra Effort and Extreme Effort from Fate System Toolkit, trading
    stress and/or consequences for bonuses.

    For even more detail, we could define the 五行 tradition as a character via the Fate Fractal.
