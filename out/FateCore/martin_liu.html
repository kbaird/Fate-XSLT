Phases
Phase 1: Your AdventureAspects: Chinese Elven Culture & Heritage 2035: Tír Tairngire secedes from SSC; CFS kicked out of UCAS 2036: TT invades northern CFS to Redding 2041-2044: Nationalist War in China 2042: Liu's parents emigrate from Beijing to TT 2046-05-08 17:15:12 -0700: Liu born in Cara'Sir (Portland), TT Both parents are 1st Generation Han Chinese Elves who emigrated from greater Beijing to Tír Tairngire, motivated by the welcoming-to-metahumans rhetoric of TT's founding and the unrest in China at that time. Under Tír Tairngire's autocratic government, Martin was a member of the Rinelle ke'Tesrae's moderate/liberal wing, favoring democratic elections and a reduction/elimination of the formal caste system. Since the elections of 2065, he eschews revolutionary action in favor of "within the system" reforms. He has no particular admiration for the previous unelected Princes, and adheres to no significant racism directed at either metatypes or ethnic backgrounds, thereby opposing the Elf supremacist factions in the Rinelle [LOP7-8/SofNA142-3]. His family spoke mainly 普通话 at home. His true native language is now Sperethiel, and he has a strong non-native command of English.
Phase 2: Crossing PathsAspects: SINner citizen (& Border Patrol veteran) of Tír Tairngire 2064: Liu undergoes Rite of Passage [TT57]. For the Bridging portion, he went to Anthony Lakes, Whitman National Forest in Northeastern Tír Tairngire. Tír Tairngirenes can be issued their standard Peace Force survival knife before deferred military service so they can use it in the Bridging, as Liu did. For the Naming portion, he chose to replace his birth name Resperentish with Imiritish ke'Tolosa, and made appropriate democratic reforms to the usual declarations of loyalty, cf. Reconstructionist Judaic bar mitzvah. 2065: 1st open TT elections, Larry Zincan elected High Prince. Liu voted for him. 2067: PCC absorbs Ute nation w/much Ute unrest 2068: Martin gets B.A. in Applied Thaumaturgy from UTT in Serentaneyo (Eugene), starts mandatory 2-year military service in Border Patrol. Liu served as a Lt. in the Border Patrol due to his parents being Chivalry rank [TT59].
Phase 3: Crossing Paths AgainAspects: Find a Clever Way to Minimize Harm 2070: 2nd open TT elections, Zincan re-elected. Liu votes for him as High Prince again. Of the other Princes, he has spoken most approvingly of Jake Foster & Amy Joubert [LOP14], with some support as well for Conall Taylor [LOP15]. 4th Rite of Progression held, Liu does not participate due to extended wilderness/border assignments for his military service, which he completes later that year. 2072: M.A. in Applied Thaumaturgy from UTT in Serentaneyo (Eugene); Liu moves to Tarislar, Puyallup [Seattle126]. Lives a Low lifestyle near the hospital Deireadh an Tuartheil, "The End of the Song of the People/Tribe" [Seattle128, SS:HSLL12-13], where he volunteers with Health spells.
