Under Tír Tairngire's autocratic government, Martin was a member of the Rinelle ke'Tesrae's moderate/liberal wing, favoring democratic elections and a reduction/elimination of the formal caste system. Since the elections of 2065, he eschews revolutionary action in favor of "within the system" reforms. He has no particular admiration for the previous unelected Princes, and adheres to no significant racism directed at either metatypes or ethnic backgrounds, thereby opposing the Elf supremacist factions in the Rinelle [LOP7-8/SofNA142-3]. His family spoke mainly 普通话 at home. His true native language is now Sperethiel, and he has a strong non-native command of English.
